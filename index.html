about us

our-history

Safia's pastry shop chain is a family business whose history began with amateur
pastry chef Muhayo Ayupova. Her hard work and love of home baking prompted
her to go beyond the family kitchen in 2001 and start baking for sale, first for
relatives, friends, and neighbors. Her husband, Akmal Ayupov, supports Muhayo's
creative endeavors and organizes the finished products' delivery.
